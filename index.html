What Is Livie?

Livie is an AI-powered coach designed to guide you through daily check-ins, micro-lessons, mood tracking, and tiny actionable steps—so you can build self-awareness, healthy routines, and a positive self-image without overwhelm.

What Livie Does

  • Morning Check-In: One-word mood + 1-sentence reflection + a 1-minute grounding exercise.
  • Routine & Tasks: Build your day with categories (Chores, Self-Care, Tough Days, etc.) and visual ticks.
  • Personal Course: Track long-term goals and get your next micro-lesson.
  • Mood Tracker: Log daily mood scores, tags, and notes—then spot patterns over time.
  • Soundscape: On-demand ambient audio sessions (focus, calm, energy, sleep).
  • Evening Reflection: Three journal prompts + gratitude practice.
  • Mini-Tests: Quick self-assessments (procrastination, self-esteem, DASS-21, and more).

Who It’s For

Whether you’re seeking a non-clinical companion to:

  • Build consistent self-care routines
  • Understand and manage your emotions
  • Track mood and mental well-being over time
  • Stay motivated with tiny daily wins
  • Practice mindfulness anytime, anywhere

Livie adapts to your pace and meets you where you are.
